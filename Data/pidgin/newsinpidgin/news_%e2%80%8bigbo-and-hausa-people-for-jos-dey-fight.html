​IGBO AND HAUSA PEOPLE FOR JOS DEY FIGHT

By
Deborah Jimmy
-
September 15, 2017
390
0
Facebook
Twitter
Google+
Pinterest
WhatsApp

    With d kind gbege wey dey happen for east, north sef wan follow start dia own. Gbege bin happen yesterday as Igbo and Hausa people wey dey live for Jos kon get clash.
    D Premium Times report s matter talk say na unto small disagreement wey happen between some Hausa and Igbo people wey dey live for Rwang Pam street and d argument bin be unto d violence for Abia.

    One witness talk say d police don take over the Rwang Pam street and Ahmadu Bello way.

    D witness yan say;

    “Everything dey under control now as everyone don dey rush go back dia house,”

    Another witness wey dey live d area sef follow talk say d situation don dey calm as at now.

    “D situation dey calm now, we fit see police everywhere.”

    D police for dat side just get new commissioner and him just start duty, and him never not release any statement unto d matter as at now.
